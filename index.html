How to play Jacks!

Jacks is a solo card game where the goal is to get to the end of the deck of cards. To get to the end of the deck, you must click on two cards that add to 11, or a king, queen and jack. If there are no options left, you lose the game. The game is based fully on luck, so don't feel down if you lose. Good Luck!
